# 🎵 Spotify_Music_Analysis

The Spotify Music Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Power BI dashboard that provides comprehensive insights into Spotify listening behavior. It analyzes streaming activity, listening duration, artists, tracks, completion status, and listening patterns across different time periods. The dashboard transforms raw streaming data into meaningful visualizations, enabling users to understand their music consumption habits and identify listening trends.

🎯 Objectives
* Analyze overall Spotify listening activity.
* Track streaming trends over time.
* Identify the most-streamed artists and tracks.
* Measure listening duration and engagement.
* Analyze skip rate and completion behavior.
* Discover listening patterns by month, weekday, and time of day.
* Build an interactive dashboard for music analytics.

 # 📈 Dashboard Insights
 
### 🎧 Listening Activity
* Over 149K total streams were analyzed.
* More than 320K listening minutes (approximately 5.3K hours) were recorded.
* Users listened to around 4,000 unique artists and 14,000 unique tracks.
### 🌅 Time-Based Listening
* Morning listening accounts for the highest number of streams.
* Night is the second most active listening period.
* Afternoon has comparatively fewer streams.
### 📅 Monthly Trends
* Streaming activity varies across months, with certain months showing higher engagement.
* Total listening hours generally follow the overall streaming trend.
### 🎤 Artist Performance
* The Beatles appear among the most frequently streamed artists.
* Artists such as The Killers, John Mayer, and Bob Dylan also contribute significantly to total streams.
### 🎵 Track Performance
* A small number of tracks account for a large share of total listening time.
* Most tracks receive relatively fewer streams, indicating a long-tail listening pattern.
### ⏭️ User Engagement
* The majority of songs are played to completion.
* Skip rate remains low (around 5%), suggesting strong listener engagement.

# 📌 Conclusion

The Spotify Music Analysis Dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of music listening behavior by transforming raw Spotify streaming data into meaningful, interactive insights. Through a combination of KPIs, charts, and visualizations, the dashboard enables users to explore streaming patterns, artist popularity, track performance, listening duration, and user engagement.

The analysis reveals that listening activity is highest during the morning, with strong engagement reflected by a low skip rate and a high song completion percentage. It also highlights the most-streamed artists and tracks while uncovering trends across months, weekdays, and time periods. These insights help users better understand their listening habits and identify preferences over time.

From a technical perspective, this project demonstrates practical skills in Power BI, including data cleaning with Power Query, data modeling, DAX calculations, interactive dashboard design, and storytelling with data. It showcases how business intelligence tools can transform large datasets into actionable insights through effective visualization.

Overall, the Spotify Music Analysis Dashboard serves as a valuable analytics solution for understanding music consumption patterns and user engagement, making it an excellent portfolio project that demonstrates end-to-end data analytics and business intelligence capabilities.
